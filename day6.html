Swami vivekananda

1863-1902

"Never say NO, Never say, 'I cannot', for you are INFINITE. All the power is WITHIN you. You can do anything."

biography

Once Swami Vivekananda was sleeping in his ashram. That's why a person came to him who was very sad and as soon as he came, he fell at the feet of Swami Vivekananda and said, Maharaj, I work very hard in my life, I do everything with a lot of heart, yet till today I am never a successful person. Was able to become After listening to that person, Swami Vivekananda said it is okay. You take this pet dog of mine for a while till then I will find a solution to your problem. After saying this, the person went to walk the dog. And then after some time passed that person came back. So Swami Vivekananda asked that person why this dog is panting so much.
